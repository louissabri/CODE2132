Facade Inspiration

My facade design draws direct inspiration from the images on the Pinterest board. The interplay of light and shadow created by the metal fins in those images captivated me. I sought to recreate that dynamic shading effect in my design, ensuring the building not only looks aesthetically appealing but also provides functional shading.

The metal fins give the facade a sleek, modern look. Their vertical alignment and subtle curves add a sense of fluidity and movement, much like the examples I found on Pinterest. These fins create an ever-changing visual experience as the sun moves, enhancing the building's aesthetic appeal throughout the day.
